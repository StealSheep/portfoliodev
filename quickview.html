Web app design

Applying design thinking to internal apps

The QuickView application at W. W. Norton was a heavily-used and much-relied-upon web app that had been in use since the early 2000's. However, it had been built by developers using default ASP.Net controls and the focus had clearly been on functionality and not the overall user experience.

Given that this was a mission-critical app accessed thousands of times a day by sales and production staff it needed some love. So, our staff spent some time speaking with a select group of users and came up with a punch-list of improvements we needed to make. In addition to applying an actual visual design, responsive and accessibility rules were applied with an emphasis on the most-used tools according to the users.
