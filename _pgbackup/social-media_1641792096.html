Social media

Raising awareness

A selection of social media assets created by me and my team for UBS.

Given that this was a mission-critical app accessed thousands of times a day by sales and production staff it needed some love. So our staff spent some time speaking with a select group of users and came up with a punch-list of improvements we needed to make. In addition to applying an actual visual design, reponsive and accessibility rules were applied with an emphasis on the most-used tools according to the users.
