Emails

Reinforcing the brand

In an effort to bring consistent brand application to our users' entire journey, we undertook a major effort to redesign all of UBS' email communications applying the same brand rules that we used for the web properties.

UBS uses at least four different unconnected platforms to deliver email and they were all independelty designed without regard to brand rules---or at least, rules that were long out of date. We redeisnged all email communications, firm-wide, to match current brand standards, making the emails responsive and modern, accessible and attractive across all email clients, yet flexible enough to be used by the myriad complex CMPs used across the firm.
