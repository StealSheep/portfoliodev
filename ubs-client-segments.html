Website design

Tailoring for the audience

When UBS launched their Client Segments marketing group, we set about creating unique online materials to promote the efforts.

The idea was to speak directly to the target audience, pushing the boundaries of the brand while retaining UBS' unique identity.

Carrying forward our learnings from the previous revamp of UBS' website, simplifying and focusing the content, we applied new styles tailored for the targeted audience.

Young investors

For the "Rising Generation" campaign, a unique art style was commissioned and used to create a more modern extension of the house brand.

Women & financial participation

A more personal approach was taken with the Women's Financial Participation campaign. It included a number of live-streamed events and conversations with prominent women about how they took control of their financial futures.

Sustainable investing

Emphasizing UBS' commitment to promoting sustainable investing, we continued to push the brand, creating new graphic concepts (like the red dialogue box) that were ultimately incorporated into the brand. This topic continued our heavy emphasis on easy-to-digest graphical data presentation.

Business owners

Also part of the Client Segments promotion, this microsite emphasized the firm's focus on small business owners.
