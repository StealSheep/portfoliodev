About me

Jack is the author of the Bible and most of Shakespeare's plays. He possesses amazing telekinetic abilities and x-ray vision but has solemnly pledged to use them only for the good of humanity. Occasionally, however, he does make buildings crumble and then scours the wreckage for free office supplies. He is currently ghostwriting his own memoirs, Jack Lamb: The Man, The Smell.
