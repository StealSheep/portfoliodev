Website/platform design

A project becomes a platform

Author Michael Lewis was already extremely active online but had no central hub for his work. His publisher, W. W. Norton offered to provide him with a simple but effective website that not only promoted the author and his work, but served as a central hub for his prolific online writing for Vanity Fair and other publications.

Visit the live site

When the site was successfully launched we began to get inquiries from other authors about providing the same service. Rather than reinvent the wheel, we used the foundation of Lewis' site to create a system for authors to quickly get a web presence online.
